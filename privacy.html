Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

link ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by Jaskaran Cheema. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the link mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, school email address (.edu), class year, major, and affiliation/club.

Profile Information: You may optionally upload a profile photo.

Content You Create: Posts, photos, comments, events, and direct messages you create within the App.

Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the App, such as features used and actions taken.

Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for push notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To provide App features including posts, events, messaging, and friend connections
  • To verify your student status via your .edu email address
  • To assign you to your campus community based on your email domain
  • To send push notifications about activity relevant to you
  • To improve and maintain the App

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your profile information (name, year, major, affiliation) is visible to other users within the App. Private accounts limit visibility of activity to approved friends only.

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: We use Google Firebase for authentication, data storage, and hosting. Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which encrypt data at rest and in transit. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Edit: You can view and edit your profile information within the App at any time.
  • Delete Your Account: You can delete your account from the Profile section of the App. This permanently removes your account data from our systems.
  • Data Request: You may request a copy of your data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Age Requirement

The App is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a user under 18, we will delete that information promptly.

7. Campus Communities

Your campus community is determined by your .edu email domain. Events and certain content are scoped to your campus. You may interact with users from other campuses through friend connections and cross-campus event invitations.

8. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications about friend requests, event updates, messages, and other App activity. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
